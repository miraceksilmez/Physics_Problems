## Phase Difference

**Given:**
- Distance between two points: \( \frac{\lambda}{3} \)

**Formula:**
\[
\Delta \phi = \frac{2\pi}{\lambda} \cdot \Delta x
\]

**Substitute:**
\[
\Delta \phi = \frac{2\pi}{\lambda} \cdot \frac{\lambda}{3}
\]

**Simplify:**
\[
\Delta \phi = \frac{2\pi}{3}
\]

**Final Answer:**
\[
\boxed{\frac{2\pi}{3} \text{ radians}}
\]
