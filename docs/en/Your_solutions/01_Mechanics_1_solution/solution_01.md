## 1. Projectile Motion — Step by Step

### Given:
\[
v_0 = 100 \text{ m/s},\quad \theta = 37^\circ,\quad g = 9.8 \text{ m/s}^2
\]

---

### Step 1: Velocity Components
\[
v_{0x} = v_0 \cos\theta = 100 \cos 37^\circ \approx 80 \text{ m/s}
\]

\[
v_{0y} = v_0 \sin\theta = 100 \sin 37^\circ \approx 60 \text{ m/s}
\]

---

### Step 2: Differential Equations

Horizontal:
\[
\frac{d^2 x}{dt^2} = 0
\]

Vertical:
\[
\frac{d^2 y}{dt^2} = -g
\]

---

### Step 3: Integrate (Velocity)

\[
v_x = 80
\]

\[
v_y = 60 - gt = 60 - 9.8t
\]

---

### Step 4: Integrate (Position)

\[
x(t) = 80t
\]

\[
y(t) = 60t - \frac{1}{2}gt^2 = 60t - 4.9t^2
\]

---

### Step 5: Time of Flight

Set \( y = 0 \):

\[
0 = 60t - 4.9t^2
\Rightarrow t(60 - 4.9t)=0
\]

\[
t = \frac{60}{4.9} \approx 12.24 \text{ s}
\]

---

### Step 6: Maximum Height

At peak \( v_y = 0 \):

\[
60 - 9.8t = 0 \Rightarrow t = \frac{60}{9.8} \approx 6.12
\]

\[
H = 60(6.12) - 4.9(6.12)^2 \approx 184 \text{ m}
\]

---

### Step 7: Range

\[
R = v_{0x} \cdot T = 80 \cdot 12.24 \approx 979 \text{ m}
\]
